Starting Point

My journey began in physics and engineering. When I was young, I always thought my dream was to be a scientist who can contribute to the development of technology, which makes life better for humans. This dream drove me to finish my Physics MPhil in university and to be an engineer in the early years of my career.

After years, I felt bored of repeated, routine jobs, and I recognized my desire to change, and to broaden my horizons.

Something does not change, I sought to understand how the world works and find ways to improve lives through technology.

Turning Point

Dramatically, my serious illness acted as the ignition point: I was forced to take a long rest, but it helped me discover new passions and consider alternative career paths I might not have otherwise explored.

I started self-learning coding in VBA.

Ignition Point

When I returned to my original work duty, I clicked the auto program to process boring copy and paste data sheet tasks.

The instant it finished tasks ignited my passion for coding.

I was impressed how it boosted my job efficiency, it was satisfying, and its possibilities.

New Exploration

I continued improving my coding skill, exploring new possible more efficient solutions.

I really enjoy the process. Meanwhile, I was looking for a way to start a new career of being a software developer.

Finally, I got a job in a technical solution vendor company, being an analyst programmer.

This is the starting point of my new adventure.
